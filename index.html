Execution Guardrails

Non-Negotiable Rules

✅ MUST DO

  • Dedicate 2 hours of focused, deep-work execution daily, without exception.
  • Ship at least 1 public GitHub repository or hosted demo per week.
  • Send 5 highly customized cold pitches every weekday to pre-qualified leads.
  • Document your build process and share systems value publicly (LinkedIn / X).

❌ MUST AVOID

  • Do not get trapped in Tutorial Hell—build, break, and debug real systems.
  • Never negotiate your rates down below minimum sustainable standards; select clients carefully.
  • Avoid spending hours on admin chores—prioritize building and pitching daily.
  • Do not break consistency. Never skip execution for two consecutive days under any circumstance.
